Tutorial 1 - Challenge: Calculator

Now it is time to use the things you have learned. Use what you have learned from the previous tutorials to make a calculator that adds and subtracts number.

It should take three inputs (a, b and c).

The output should be "(a)+(b)-(c)=" and on the next line the answer.

When the inputs are
24, 53, 37
the output should be
24+53-37=
40
