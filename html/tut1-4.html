Tutorial 1 - 4: Other Math

The .code language is not only limited to addition. Subtraction, multiplication and division are also possible. These operations are used in the exact same way that adition is done:
subtract (a) 56 32 (56 - 32)
multiply (b) 3 9 (3 * 9)
divide (c) 49 7 (49 / 7)

Try writing a program like you did for addition but this time using different mathematical operations.
